How To Make Banners

  • Create an account on Canva.
  • Create a new design (purple button, top left).
  • Click on set custom dimensions, and create a new 900x200.
  • Create the banner, using a template (if you want), adding a background, text, elements and more.
  • Add credit: Make small text in one corner saying that their banner is from -gr's banner shop.
  • Click animate. Select an animation that you see fit and add it.
  • Post the banner in the forum topic and notify them on their profile that their banner is ready.
  • Give yourself a pat on the back.
